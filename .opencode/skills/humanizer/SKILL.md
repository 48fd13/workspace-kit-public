# Humanizer

Rewrite text so it sounds natural and specific while preserving meaning and facts.

## Watch for

- generic conclusions
- inflated significance
- excessive em dashes or bold formatting
- formulaic transitions
- vague attributions
- rule-of-three phrasing
- chatbot artifacts
- promotional or bureaucratic language

## Process

1. Identify the intended audience and tone.
2. Preserve facts and claims.
3. Remove AI-sounding structure and filler.
4. Vary sentence rhythm.
5. Prefer specific, plain language.
6. Do a final pass for anything that still sounds generated.
