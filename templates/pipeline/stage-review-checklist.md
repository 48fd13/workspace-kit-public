# Stage Review Checklist

Before moving to the next stage:

- [ ] Output exists under `_workspace/runs/active/<run-slug>/stages/`
- [ ] Output follows the stage contract
- [ ] Required inputs were used
- [ ] Reference constraints were followed
- [ ] No unsupported facts were introduced
- [ ] Decisions from previous stages were preserved
- [ ] Open questions or risks are explicit
- [ ] Human reviewed or approved the output
