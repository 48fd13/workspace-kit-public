# Runbook Draft Workflow

## Required context

- `_workspace/context/reference/incident-and-oncall-principles.md`
- `_workspace/context/reference/observability-principles.md`

## Process

1. Identify symptom or operational task.
2. Capture safe checks, decision tree, escalation, mitigation, validation, and recovery.
3. Mark commands as read-only or mutation-gated.
4. Save runbook under `_workspace/outputs/runbooks/`.
