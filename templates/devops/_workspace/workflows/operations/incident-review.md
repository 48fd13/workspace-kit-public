# Incident Review Workflow

Use for quick incident/alert reviews that do not need the full incident-learning pipeline.

## Required context

- `_workspace/context/reference/incident-and-oncall-principles.md`
- `_workspace/context/reference/observability-principles.md`

## Process

1. Build a timeline from facts only.
2. Separate impact, symptoms, mitigations, confirmed causes, hypotheses, and open questions.
3. Identify action items and runbook improvements.
4. Save incident note under `_workspace/outputs/incident-notes/`.
