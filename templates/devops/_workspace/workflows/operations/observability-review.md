# Observability Review Workflow

## Required context

- `_workspace/context/reference/observability-principles.md`
- `_workspace/context/reference/tool-notes/observability.md`
- `_workspace/runbooks/observability.md`

## Process

1. Identify service/environment and available logs, metrics, traces, dashboards, alerts.
2. Check whether signals answer operational questions.
3. Review alert actionability, ownership, thresholds, and runbook links.
4. Save review under `_workspace/outputs/reviews/`.
