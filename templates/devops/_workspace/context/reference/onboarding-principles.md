# Onboarding Principles

Use onboarding whenever the project/team/service/environment scope changes.

Capture:

- service purpose and criticality
- owners and escalation paths
- environments and deployment flow
- cloud accounts/projects/subscriptions
- infrastructure/IaC boundaries
- CI/CD paths and approvals
- observability and incident touchpoints
- known risks and open questions

Do not pretend uncertain information is fact. Mark unknowns and produce targeted questions.
