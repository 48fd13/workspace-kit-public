# Incident and On-call Principles

- Separate facts from hypotheses.
- Capture timeline before explaining cause.
- Mitigation and root cause are different.
- Prefer actionable signals over noisy dashboards.
- Record what was checked, what was ruled out, and why.
- Produce runbook improvements from repeated confusion or manual recovery.
- Avoid blame. Focus on system behavior, detection, mitigation, and prevention.
